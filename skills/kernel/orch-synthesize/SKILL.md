---
name: orch-synthesize
description: Combine independent evidence packets into one traced synthesis. Use as the research assembly step or any evidence join.
role: planner
---

Require: two or more evidence packets, each stating its sources;
independence of converging claims is judged at the claim level, never
assumed at the packet level.

Combine on the claims, not the prose: every synthesized claim traces to
the packet and source that support it. Where packets disagree, register
the disagreement with both sides' evidence — never average it away and
never pick a winner without stating why. Name the gaps the packets
jointly leave open.

Never: introduce a claim no packet carries; hide a lane's failure inside
a smooth narrative; collapse disagreement without evidence.

Return: the synthesis, a claim-to-source trace, the disagreement
register, and gaps.
