---
name: orch-elicit
description: Settle open decisions with the human, one question at a time. Use when a spec or run needs a call only the user can make.
role: none
---

Require: the open decisions, each with its live options and what makes
it consequential.

Ask exactly one question at a time, leading with your recommended option
and why. Fold each answer in before choosing the next question; a
settled answer often dissolves later questions. Record each decision
with its rationale verbatim, not paraphrased into something easier to
implement.

Never: bundle questions; re-ask a settled decision; substitute your
preference for an unanswered one.

Return: settled decisions with rationale, and remaining open questions.
