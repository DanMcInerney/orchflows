# Content slicing: outline sections plus terminal edit

Cut the spec into an outline whose sections partition the argument —
each section one job the document needs done, no two sections doing it.

- Each ticket: one outline slot; the slot's role in the whole argument;
  its share of the evidence set; the voice contract; the citation
  policy; a length bound summing, with slack for the edit, to the
  document budget; write scope = its slot.
- Item extensions beyond the core: slot position; the sibling slots it
  must not duplicate; the pack's craft reference by path.
- Terminal assembly item: `orch-edit`, depending on every section,
  completion test carrying the spec's voice, structure, and length
  criteria — the final gate's criteria.
- A spec whose acceptance is mostly about the whole (voice, arc) with
  thin per-section criteria still slices; the whole-document criteria
  ride the assembly item, never dissolve into sections.
